Planning

Every trip beggins with desire. There are places we dream of seeing before we die and people we want to catch up with at the end od lockdown. Whether its a single journey or many, the first thing you need is a plan. TravelBug aims to provide users with a simple clean interface to plan each of your trips, whether it be a visit to the country or a world-wide tour of destiny. Select your destination and plan your travel routes and itenary. Save each trip at any time and come back to it when you have more ideas. Not quite sure where to go yet? Create your very own Travel Inspiration Board (feature coming soon) to pin photos of places and things you would like to do. Our mobile friendly app lets you plan your dream trips and add inspiration while you are travelling to work or on a coffee break. You can also invite family, friends and colleagues to join your trip and enjoy planning your next trip together.

Travelling

Planning should lead to doing! TravelBug encourages users to convert their dreams into reality by submitting their trip to a travel agent for a full curation. TravelBug's uthorised agents can send qhotes for the cost of your journey and users can choose to book and pay on the spot. Committing to the journey is the best way to make it happen.

Sharing

One of the best parts about travelling is the memories and sharing your experiences with others. TraveBug lets users connect socially (feature coming soon) and share their completed journeys for others to see and comment on. TravelBug also includes a personal Travel Blog (feature coming soon) that lets you keep track of your experiences along the way with words and images. Users can choose to keep their Blog as a private travel diary or make it public to share with other members.
